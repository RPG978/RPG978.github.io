# Android Weight Tracker app
## Artifact Summary

This app was designed to help users track their weight over time, set personal goals and visualize progress in a clean, 
accessible interface. It addresses the need for a private, ad-free and intuitive tool that empowers users to manage their 
health without distractions or data exploitation.
